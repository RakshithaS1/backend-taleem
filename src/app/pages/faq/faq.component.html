What is Junub Academy and who are the educators?

Junub Academy is an online learning platform offering a wide range of courses taught by experienced educators and industry professionals. Our educators are carefully vetted experts in their respective fields, passionate about sharing knowledge and helping learners succeed.

How do I enroll in a course?

To enroll, simply browse our course catalog, select the course you’re interested in, and click the “Enroll Now” button. You’ll be guided through the registration and payment process.

Are the courses self-paced or instructor-led?

Most of our courses are self-paced, allowing you to learn at your own speed. Some courses may offer live sessions or instructor support, which will be indicated in the course details.

Do I get a certificate after completing a course?

Yes, upon successful completion of a course, you will receive a certificate of completion that you can download and share on your resume or LinkedIn profile.

What if I have questions during the course?

You can ask questions in the course discussion forums or contact the instructor directly if the course offers instructor support. Our support team is also available to assist you with any issues.

Can I access the course materials after completion?

Yes, you will have lifetime access to the course materials, so you can revisit the content anytime even after completing the course.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept major credit/debit cards and other secure online payment methods. All transactions are encrypted and secure.
